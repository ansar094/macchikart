Services We Provide

Fresh Fish

Every morning we collect the day’s catch from trusted local fishermen. The fish is cleaned, packed in ice, and delivered the same day so it reaches you fresh and full of flavor. Just place your order in the app and enjoy market-fresh seafood without leaving home.

Dry Fish

For longer-lasting taste, we also prepare dry fish in our own specially designed machines. The fish is dried carefully and hygienically to keep its natural flavor while making it easy to store. Choose your favorites and we’ll bring clean, ready-to-cook dry fish straight to your door.

About Us

Who We Are
Macchikart is a local seafood company dedicated to bringing the best fish from the morning catch straight to your home. We work closely with trusted fishermen to ensure every fish is fresh, clean, and handled with care. From chilled fresh fish to hygienically dried fish using our own specially designed machines, we make sure you get quality seafood without the hassle of visiting the market.

What We Do
At Macchikart, we deliver fresh fish straight from the boats every morning and also prepare hygienically dried fish using our specially designed machines. Whether you want fish for today’s meal or ready-to-store dry fish, we handle everything carefully and bring it straight to your doorstep, making seafood easy, safe, and convenient for every household.

Our Mission & Values
At Macchikart, our mission is to make fresh and safe seafood accessible to every household while supporting local fishermen and promoting hygienic, sustainable practices. We value quality, freshness, and convenience in every step—from sourcing the day’s catch to delivering fresh or carefully dried fish straight to your door. Our purpose is simple: to bring the taste of the ocean to your kitchen, responsibly and reliably.

Our Team
Machhikart was started as a college project by Atheeq and Mushviq, two 3rd-year Mechanical Engineering students at SJEC. They personally source fish from trusted local fishermen they know and handle the initial operations themselves. As the business grows, they plan to bring in partners to help with deliveries, ensuring that every order reaches customers fresh, safe, and ready to enjoy.
